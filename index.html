01 About

The signals that drive defence deals, verified and in one place.

Today, sourcing investment into defence is manual and network-based. Investors truly only care about the founders, the company's legal structure, and the operational KPIs of the products, and those signals aren't in the generalist deal-sourcing tools. RaisePoint is here to help, with a unified, defence-focused research and analysis tool.

New companies and signals appear here first

Our algorithm quickly captures and verifies fresh data, then structures it into easy-to-analyse output.

Signals that move deals

Combat validation, cross-border legal structure, founder-network depth and headcount growth and others. Checked against sourced evidence.

Smart company scoring system

Every company carries a single RaisePoint score that blends the most relevant weighted signals.

Analyse company-investor compatibility

See which investors that you follow already back a company, and see how your own portfolio fits with each potential target.

02 Security

A private database, by design.

The companies we cover trust us with sensitive information, so we treat access as a privilege, not a default. Every user is identity-verified and vetted before they see a single record, and we never publish anything that could put a company at risk. Privacy isn't a setting on RaisePoint. It's the reason the data exists.

04 FAQ

Got questions?
We have the answers.

How is this different from our competitors?
Generalist platforms are broad but shallow on defence. RaisePoint is defence-only and Europe/Ukraine-deep, built bottom-up to include early companies they don't list, and organised around the signals that actually help to analyse defence deals.
What's in a tearsheet?
Verified funding history, investors, products, battle-validation evidence with sources, legal structure, a founder-network overview, and a RaisePoint score.
What is the RaisePoint score?
A single number per company that blends a handful of weighted signals under a proprietary methodology.
Who can get access?
Qualified funds and advisors with a defence or Ukraine/EU mandate. Access is invite-led and granted to a small number of firms at a time.
